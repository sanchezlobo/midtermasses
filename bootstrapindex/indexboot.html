Top Trails in SOCAL

Welcome to our guide to the top trails in Southern California. Whether you're seeking scenic vistas, challenging terrain, or serene waterfalls, these trails have something for everyone.

Top Trails

Hollywood Sign Trail

Enjoy a 6-mile hike to the iconic Hollywood Sign, offering breathtaking views of Los Angeles.

  • LEVEL OF DIFFICULTY: 3
  • FEATURES: Close View of Hollywood Sign, View of LA CITY
  • MILES: 6

Devils PunchBowl Trail

Explore the unique rock formations along this 7.5-mile trail, offering stunning views of mountain peaks.

  • LEVEL OF DIFFICULTY: 2
  • FEATURES: Unique Rocks, View of Mountain Peaks
  • MILES: 7.5

Sturtevant Falls Trail

Experience a scenic 3.3-mile hike to a stunning hidden waterfall, surrounded by wildflowers.

  • LEVEL OF DIFFICULTY: 2
  • FEATURES: Waterfall, Wildflowers, Kid-friendly
  • MILES: 3.3

Benefits of Hiking

Hiking offers numerous benefits for physical and mental health. It provides a great cardiovascular workout, strengthens muscles, and reduces stress levels. Additionally, hiking allows you to connect with nature and explore beautiful landscapes, promoting overall well-being.
